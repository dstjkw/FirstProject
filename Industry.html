FASHION INDUSTRY



The fashion industry is always looking for the latest trends to absorb, reimagine, and then renew relationships with the rest of the world in its own way.
This behavior is also common in web design. Thus, at the junction of the worlds of fashion and the Internet, sites appear that combine the latest
visual and technological trends.


Each fashion brand strives to create a unique and distinctive visual style that matches its personality. However, there are certain characteristics that are common
to the websites of most fashion companies. Most fashion sites rely on visuals to grab attention and let the products speak for themselves.
The use of photographs is imperative, large title images and videos are very popular.In most cases, the home page consists of a large background photo and a navigation menu.
The rest of the content is pushed into the background so it does not compete with the products displayed.


Black and white is a common color scheme among trendy websites, especially those looking to convey an image of elegance and sophistication.
In contrast, young and playful brands prefer to use a lot more colors.
